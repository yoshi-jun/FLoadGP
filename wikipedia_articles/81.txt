    From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia   Turkish actress   Hazal AdıyamanBorn (1989-08-06) 6 August 1989 (age 35)Adapazarı, TurkeyNationalityTurkishOccupationActressYears active2013–presentHeight172 cm (5 ft 8 in) Hazal Adıyaman (born 6 August 1989) is a Turkish actress best known for her roles as 'Gül' in Muhteşem Yüzyıl, 'Derin Çelik' in Bizim Hikaye, and 'Princess Adelfa' in Kuruluş: Osman.   Life and career[edit] Adıyaman has a bachelor's degree in physics from Marmara University. She started her career as an actress in 2013 when she played a recurring role as Gül on the series Muhteşem Yüzyıl.[1] She then played the role of Melek on an episode of the series Aşkın Bedeli in the same year. Adıyaman gained attention when she starred in the drama series Bizim Hikaye by playing the role of Derin Çelik, the younger sister of Deniz Çelik (portrayed by Melisa Döngel).[2] In 2020, she appeared as Princess Adelfa in Kuruluş: Osman.[3] Adıyaman is also known in the Turkish media for her natural beauty.[4]  Filmography[edit] Television series[edit]   Year  Title  Role  Channel  Notes  Episodes   2013  Bir Yusuf Masali  Yasemin         2013  Cost of Love  Melek         2013  The Magnificent Century  Gül         2014  Cherry Season  Arzu         2014  Gönül Isleri           2016  Hanim Koylu  Gulgun         2016 2018   Deniz Inside My Heart  Ece Öztuna         2018 2019   Bizim Hikaye  Derin         2020  Kurulus: Osman  Adelfa         2021  Uzak Sehrin Masali           2022  Yalniz Kurt  Kübra        Film[edit]   Year  Title  Role  Notes   2013 Muhteşem Yüzyıl Gül     Aşkın Bedeli Melek     2014 Bir Yusuf Masali Yasemin     2014–2015 Kiraz Mevsimi -  Guest appearance   2016 Hanım Köylü Gülgün     2017 Kalbimdeki Deniz Ece     2018–2019 Bizim Hikaye Derin Çelik  Main character   2020 Kuruluş: Osman Princess Adelfa  Guest appearance  References[edit]   ^ "Kuruluş Osman Bizans Prensesi Adelfa karakterini canlandıran Hazal Adıyaman kimdir, kaç yaşında?
