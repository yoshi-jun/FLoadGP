    From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia   Tennis tournamentXII Venice Challenge Save CupDate2–8 June 2014Edition1stDraw32S / 16DPrize money€42,500SurfaceClayLocationMestre, ItalyChampionsSingles Pablo CuevasDoubles Pablo Cuevas /  Horacio Zeballos   Venice Challenge Save Cup · XIII →  The XII Venice Challenge Save Cup was a professional tennis tournament played on clay courts. It was the 1st edition of the men's tournament which was part of the 2014 ATP Challenger Tour. It took place in Mestre, Italy between 2 and 8 June 2014.
