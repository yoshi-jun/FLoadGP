Lines[edit] Kita Shin-Yokohama Station is served by the Yokohama Municipal Subway Blue Line, and lies 30.8 km (19.1 mi) from the terminus of the line at Shōnandai Station.  Station layout[edit] The station has a single island platform serving two tracks.
