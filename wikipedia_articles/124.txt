Lanquetot (French pronunciation: [lɑ̃kto]) is a commune in the Seine-Maritime department in the Normandy region in northern France.   Geography[edit] A farming village in the Pays de Caux, some 19 miles (31 km) northeast of Le Havre, at the junction of the D9015, D30 and D109 roads.  Heraldry[edit]   Arms of Lanquetot  The arms of Lanquetot are blazoned :Gules, in fess 2 garbs between 2 leopards respectant and 2 weaver's shuttles in saltire Or.
