It is situated in the green mountains near the border with Laconia.[2] The community consists of the main village Kollines and the small villages Achouri and Voutouchos. It is located 1 km east of Agia Varvara, 9 km south of Vlachokerasia and 25 km south of Tripoli.   Museum[edit] The cultural association Friends of Skyritida opened a folklore museum in Kollines in 1997.
