Find sources: "Holly Williams" writer – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (March 2022) (Learn how and when to remove this message) Holly Williams is a Welsh arts and features writer and theatre critic.[1][2][3][non-primary source needed]  She grew up in Wales, studied at the University of York[4] and worked for a local[clarification needed] newspaper before moving to London. From 2010 to 2016 she worked at The Independent and The Independent on Sunday.[5] Since 2016 she has been a staff writer for WhatsOnStage.com.[6] She has also contributed to The Daily Telegraph[1][2] and the London Evening Standard.[7] Since 2019, she has been a judge for the Evening Standard Theatre Awards.[8]  References[edit]   ^ a b Williams, Holly (10 May 2016).
