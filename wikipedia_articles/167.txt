As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 5,774 and an area of 30.6 square kilometres (11.8 sq mi).[3] The municipality of Remanzacco contains the frazioni (boroughs) Ziracco, Selvis, Orzano, and Cerneglons. Remanzacco borders the following municipalities: Faedis, Moimacco, Povoletto, Pradamano, Premariacco, Udine.
