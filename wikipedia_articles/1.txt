Lillemer (French pronunciation: [lilmɛʁ]; Breton: Enez-Veur) is a commune in the Ille-et-Vilaine department of Brittany in northwestern France. As of 2019, the population was 367.   Population[edit] Inhabitants of Lillemer are called in French lillemérois or lillemériens.
