(Learn how and when to remove this message)   Beatriz CostaBorn(1907-12-14)14 December 1907Mafra, PortugalDied15 April 1996(1996-04-15) (aged 88)Lisbon, PortugalOccupationactressSignature Beatriz Costa (born Beatriz da Conceição, 14 December 1907 – 15 April 1996) was a Portuguese actress, the best-known actress of the golden age of Portuguese cinema.  She was the author of several books.
