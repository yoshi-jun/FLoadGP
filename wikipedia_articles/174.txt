David & Poll 1937   Campylomormyrus luapulaensis is a species of electric fish in the family Mormyridae, found in Africa: in the Luapula River of the upper Congo River basin in Democratic Republic of the Congo  and Zambia.[2]  Size[edit] This species reaches a length of 22.0 cm (8.7 in).[3]  Etymology[edit] The fish is named in honor of the upper Luapula River, Congo River basin, Zaire now Democratic Republic of the Congo.
