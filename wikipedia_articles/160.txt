    From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia   Subgenus of reptiles     Varanus (Philippinosaurus)   Scientific classification    Domain:  Eukaryota   Kingdom:  Animalia   Phylum:  Chordata   Class:  Reptilia   Order:  Squamata   Family:  Varanidae   Genus:  Varanus   Subgenus:  Philippinosaurus   Species    Varanus bitatawa Varanus mabitang Varanus olivaceus   Philippinosaurus is one of the 11 subgenera of the genus Varanus. The group consists of three frugivorous species endemic to the Philippines.[1]  Feeding habits[edit] Unlike other members of the Varanus genus, species belonging to Philippinosaurus are  frugivorous. While V. bitatawa and V. olivaceus also supplement their diet of fruit with insects and snails, V. mabitang feeds exclusively on fruit.
