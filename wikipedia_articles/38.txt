According to the 2002 census, the village has a population of 519 people.[1]  Infrastracture[edit] The village contains a church dedicated to Saints Peter and Paul built in 1618.  History[edit] In 1942, during the German occupation of Serbia, the German occupiers, in a punitive expedition, imprisoned dozens of local men, women and children in the church, after which they blew it up with the people still inside.
